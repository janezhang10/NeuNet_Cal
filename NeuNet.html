Make neural networks with your voice!

General

Use your voice to build Neural Networks!

NeuNet is a rapid model prototyping tool. Simply describe the parameters of your model in plain English and NeuNet will build and train the network for you!

Example

‘Give me a UNet with input size of 192 pixels and 4 output classes. Use ReLU nonlinearities and train the model on 25 epochs of the buildings dataset.'

Technical

Uses the Google Speech-to-text API to convert voice to text and utilizes language processing techniques to extract the semantic meaning of requested model.

Built a wrapper around PyTorch to dynamically construct Neural Networks given a set of hyperparameters.
